Privacy Policy

1. Information We Collect

CryptoDailyMint collects data to improve security, performance, and user experience. Information we may collect includes:

  • Account Information: Username, referral code, and login timestamps.
  • Wallet Details: USDT wallet addresses used for deposits or withdrawals.
  • Device & Technical Logs: Browser type, device model, IP address, operating system, login locations, and security fingerprints.
  • Usage Analytics: Click behavior, page navigation, time spent on features, deposit/withdrawal actions.
  • Optional Verification Data: If the system flags an account, we may request identity confirmation for fraud prevention.

All information is collected solely to provide secure and high-quality service. We never collect unnecessary or unrelated data.

2. How We Use Your Data

We use collected data strictly to support platform security, performance, and user growth. This includes:

  • Account Security: Detecting suspicious patterns, preventing unauthorized access, and verifying login authenticity.
  • Earnings & Transactions: Processing deposits, withdrawals, referral bonuses, and daily earnings distributions.
  • Platform Optimization: Improving dashboard features, wallet synchronization, and server efficiency based on user behavior.
  • Fraud Detection: Identifying multi-accounting, or unusual activity.
  • Customer Support: Helping users resolve issues quickly through data-driven support diagnostics.
  • Legal Compliance: Ensuring operations meet financial and digital asset regulations where applicable.

We do not use or process your data for any purposes unrelated to CryptoDailyMint’s services.

3. Data Protection & Security Measures

CryptoDailyMint uses enterprise-grade security technologies to safeguard your personal information. These include:

  • End-to-end encryption for all account and wallet interactions.
  • Hashing of sensitive authentication data to prevent password exposure.
  • Firewalled servers with restricted access control to prevent unauthorized system entry.
  • Automated anomaly detection that monitors and isolates suspicious account behavior.
  • Secure data segmentation to separate personal details from financial logs.

Only authorized CryptoDailyMint security personnel can access system-level data, and only when required for fraud detection or account recovery.

4. Cookies & Tracking Technologies

CryptoDailyMint uses cookies to provide a faster, safer, and more personalized experience. Cookies help with:

  • Secure Login Sessions: Keeping your account logged in without re-authentication every page.
  • Fraud Prevention: Blocking logins from unknown or unsafe devices.
  • Performance Analytics: Understanding which features users interact with most.
  • User Preferences: Saving dashboard settings and preferred themes.

You may disable cookies through your browser, but some features may not work correctly without them.

5. Third-Party Sharing

CryptoDailyMint does not sell, rent, or trade your personal information with any third party. Information is shared only in the following limited circumstances:

  • Fraud Prevention Partners: To detect malicious activity or protect the system from abuse.
  • Blockchain Networks: When processing withdrawals (wallet addresses are public by nature).
  • Legal Requests: If required by law, court order, or regulatory authority.

We never share your data with advertisers, marketers, or unrelated entities.

6. User Rights & Control Over Your Data

You retain full control over your personal data. You may request:

  • Account Deletion: Permanent removal of account data from our systems.
  • Data Export: A summary of the data we have stored related to your account.
  • Data Correction: Updating inaccurate or outdated profile information.
  • Privacy Restrictions: Limiting or disabling certain tracking features.

Requests are processed through our 24/7 support team. Identity verification may be required to protect your account.

7. Data Retention

CryptoDailyMint retains certain account information as long as necessary to:

  • Provide uninterrupted platform services
  • Meet legal and anti-fraud obligations
  • Maintain accurate earnings and transaction records

Once data becomes unnecessary or upon verified user request, it is securely deleted.

8. Policy Updates & Amendments

CryptoDailyMint may update this Privacy Policy to reflect improvements in technology, security, and platform features. If changes occur:

  • A revised version will be posted immediately on the website.
  • Continued platform use signifies acceptance of updated terms.
  • Critical updates may be announced through dashboard notifications.

We recommend reviewing this page periodically for the latest information on how we protect your data.
