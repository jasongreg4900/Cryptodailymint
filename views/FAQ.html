Can I cancel a pending withdrawal?

Yes — as long as your request is still in the initial verification stage. CryptoDailyMint processes withdrawals using an automated, multi-layer security protocol, which allows limited cancellation flexibility.

Here is how the process works:

  • Stage 1 – Smart Verification: The system reviews device fingerprints, withdrawal patterns, and network behavior. Cancellation is available during this stage.
  • Stage 2 – Blockchain Allocation: Once the system assigns funds for blockchain dispatch, cancellation is no longer possible.
  • Stage 3 – Final Broadcast: The transfer is broadcast to the blockchain network. At this point the transaction becomes irreversible.

You can easily cancel a withdrawal from your dashboard if it has not yet passed Stage 1. After that point, our automated infrastructure processes the transfer without manual intervention.
